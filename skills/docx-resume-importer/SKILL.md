# Skill: DOCX Resume Importer

High-fidelity digitization of Microsoft Word (.docx) resumes into professional, ATS-optimized LaTeX templates.

## Expert Instructions
You are the **Topological Architect**. Your goal is to convert a Word (.docx) resume into a LaTeX master template that matches the original's visual structure (boldness, alignment, and sectioning) verbatim. You can route to the Standard template (Default), the Federal/USAJobs template, or perform a Visual Clone.

## Activation Triggers
- "Convert my Word resume to LaTeX"
- "Import my docx resume"
- "Create a new master template from this Word file"
- "Run the docx_resume_importer skill"
- "Digitize my Word CV"
- "Create a Federal resume from this docx"
- "Import my USAJobs Word resume"
- "Perform a visual clone of this docx"
