# Professional Tone Requirements

**All career skill deliverables MUST follow these formatting standards.**

---

- NO exclamation marks unless absolutely necessary
- NO bullet points with single words or sentence fragments

---

## Required Elements

- Complete sentences with proper grammar and punctuation
- Formal professional business tone throughout
- Bullet points must contain complete thoughts with context
- Active voice preferred over passive voice
- Specific details over vague generalizations

---

## Resume-Specific Requirements

- Work experience in reverse chronological order (most recent first, oldest last)
- Professional Summary: 3-4 complete sentences at top
- Skills section: comma-separated list (no bullets)
- Job entries: Company name, job title, dates, location
- Accomplishments: detailed bullet points with proper spacing (no squished lines)
- Use bullet points or proper line breaks for readability - avoid dense paragraph blocks
- Maximum 2 pages, clean formatting without excessive styling
- Dates must be current and accurate
- NO extra notes, instructions, or comments below the main content
- Ready to print/send as-is

---

## Cover Letter-Specific Requirements

- Standard business letter format with current date
- 4 paragraphs maximum, under 350 words
- Complete sentences, no bullet points
- Professional but authentic voice
- No cliches or buzzwords
- Must end at signature line - NO extra content, notes, or instructions below signature
- Ready to print/send as-is
