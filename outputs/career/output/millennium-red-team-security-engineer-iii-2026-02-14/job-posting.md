## Job Description

Red Team Security Engineer III

Overview:
Millennium is proud to be part of the Markon Solutions family of companies a network of specialized organizations united in support of critical national security missions. This partnership strengthens our ability to deliver results by expanding our technical depth, operational reach, and access to a broader bench of proven experts, ensuring our customers continue to receive best-in-class cybersecurity support.

For more than two decades, Millennium has operated at the forefront of cybersecurity. Our elite team of over 300 professionals brings an unmatched record of performance across Red Team Operations, Defensive Cyber Operations, Software Engineering, and Technical Engineering. As home to the largest contingent of contracted Red Team operators supporting the Department of Defense, Millennium delivers unparalleled threat intelligence and battle-tested expertise to both DoD and federal civilian customers.

Responsibilities:
- Lead technical execution of advanced offensive cyber operations in support of Red Team missions.
- Advise government leadership on attack paths, mission risk, and outcomes.
- Serve as technical lead, mentor, and trusted advisor within NSA-certified DoD Red Team operations.
- Lead and execute complex, multi-phase red team engagements
- Design adversary emulation campaigns aligned to real-world threat actors
- Develop and maintain custom tooling, payloads, and C2 infrastructure
- Execute advanced evasion, persistence, and long-dwell operations
- Mentor entry- and mid-level operators
- Support ROE development, mission planning, and executive out-briefs
- Ensure compliance with NSA and DoD red team standards

Qualifications:
- An active Top Secret security clearance.
- Bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university in computer science, information systems, engineering, scientific, or a mathematics-intensive discipline
- A minimum of 8 years of experience in Offensive Cyber Operations or NSA-certified DoD Red Teaming
- OSCP, ROPS RT1, or CRTO certification
- Demonstrated experience leading or serving as senior operator on classified red team missions

Highly Desired Qualifications:
- Prior NAVSEA, US Navy, or weapon system cyber assessment experience
- NSA Red Team Operator (RTO) qualification
- Advanced certifications (OSEP, OSEE, GXPN, CRTO, etc.)
- Experience with Long-dwell and stealth-focused operations; EDR/AV evasion; ICS/OT or platform-centric cyber assessments
- Strong briefing and technical writing skills for senior government audiences

Business Development:
Assist with Business Development activities as required to support Millennium's strategic business objectives.

Physical Requirements:
Must be comfortable with prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer.
Must be able to lift up to 10-15 pounds at a time.

Travel Requirements: 15%
